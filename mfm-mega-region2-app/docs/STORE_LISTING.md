## Full Description (Google Play / App Store)

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) Mega Region 2 USA official app.
Stay connected with your MFM family across Texas and Florida.

LIVESTREAM
Watch services live from the MFM Mega Region 2 YouTube channel. Access streams from MFM International HQ, MFM America, and regional branches. Never miss Power Must Change Hands, Manna Water, or Sunday worship.

DAILY DEVOTIONALS
Start your day with fire! MFM-style prayer points for spiritual warfare, deliverance, and breakthrough. Includes daily Bible reading, verse of the day, and reflections.

ANONYMOUS PRAYER WALL
Submit prayer requests anonymously and pray for others in the MFM family. Your identity is never shared. Categories include healing, deliverance, breakthrough, family, favor, and more.

BRANCH DIRECTORY
Find every MFM Mega Region 2 branch across Texas and Florida. One-tap calling, email, and Google Maps directions to your nearest branch.

EVENTS & SERVICES
Stay updated with the weekly service schedule, Power Must Change Hands dates, special events, revivals, and conferences.

ANNOUNCEMENTS
Get the latest updates from Mega Region 2 leadership, including the annual prophetic declaration and important church news.
